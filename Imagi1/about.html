MISSION

We want to work such that the students get encouraged to take up technical subjects with the help of people working in the same field as well as their peers.

VISION

We envision on providing the students with the right opportunities to learn and sharpen technical skills, as a result, empowering the future of technology.

VALUES

We are committed to ensure that students all over the world get to experience technology and the way they can make it happen- “Make technology happen”.

Impact & Future

We strive to serve a diverse community of students and will continue to do so. Our Founder and CEO, Ms. Devanshi Thakkar, conducted 4 workshops in association with VEX Robotics where she was able to promote around 300 school girls in the stream of robotics and STEM careers.

We are hoping to make a positive impact on the lives of students. Fundamentals being cleared at a young age will surely help students understand the power and hold of technology.

Imagiminds is a non-profit women-led organization working towards providing opportunities and support to students (especially girls) interested in computing and technology advancement.
